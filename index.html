Abstract

Cybersecurity researchers and practitioners have determined that modern cybersecurity methods are increasingly using AI techniques. However, the cybersecurity curriculum has not been updated yet to integrate such topics or techniques. Therefore, it is imperative to find relevant topics in AI that are most commonly used within cybersecurity in order to form an explicit AI module that integrates AI concepts into cybersecurity.
